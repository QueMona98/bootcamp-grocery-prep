Trail Mix

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup pecans
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1/4 cup cashews
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1/2 cup dried cherries
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries

One thing I love about trail mix is its customizability. Feel free to modify the ingredients!


Instructions

  1. Prepare a container
  2. Pour in ingredients
  3. Mix
