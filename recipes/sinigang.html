Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 piece fresh ginger, chopped
  • 2 medium whole plum tomatoes
  • 1 pound bone-in pork chops
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 package tamarind soup base
  • 1/2 pound fresh green beans, trimmed

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Stir in onion and cook until soft
  3. Season with salt
  4. Stir in ginger, tomatoes, and pokr chops
  5. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low
  6. Stir and cook util pork turns brown
  7. Pour in water and tamarind soup base
  8. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat
  9. Cook until pork is tender
  10. Stir in green beans and cook until tender
