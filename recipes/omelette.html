Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp chives

Feel free to add any ingredients of your choosing, such as more vegetables


  1. Crack warm eggs into a bowl
  2. Add salt and blend with a fork
  3. Heat a nonstick pan over medium-high heat
  4. Add in butter
  5. Pour eggns into the center of the pan
  6. Help shape the omeletter with a spatula as is becomes more solid
  7. Loosen the omelette and lift up the far edge
  8. Fold over omelette
