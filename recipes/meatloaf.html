Meat Loaf

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef, egg, onion, and milk
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Place in a lightly greased 5x9 inch loaf pan
  5. In a separate small bowl, combine brown sugar, mustard and ketchup and mix
  6. Pour over the meatloaf
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
