French Toast

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 8 slices of challah, brioche, or white bread
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar
  2. Melt butter in a skillet
  3. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk and vanilla
  4. Pour mixture into shallow container
  5. Dip bread in egg mixture
  6. Fry slices until golden brown
  7. Flip to cook other side
