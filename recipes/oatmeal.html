Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Quick oats, steel cut oats or rolled oats
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Water

Feel free to add any ingredients of your choosing, such as fruits or nuts.


  1. Combine oats and water/milk in a 1:2 ratio
  2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes
  3. Mix any additional ingredients
