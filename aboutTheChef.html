About Me

Hi! My name is Quentin Monasterial and I'd like to thank you so much for taking the time to check out some of my favorite dishes.

While we can all agree that food is essential to our functioning as a human being, I also believe that food is essential in many other regards. My primary reason for chosing to share some of these dishes is to share with you a piece of my identity and culture. In this sense, food is often a gateway into what lays beyond our superficial idenetities.

Background

I was born in Southern California on October 28, 1998. I lived there for about 2 years before moving up to the Bay Area where I grew up and still live. More specifically, I grew up in San Leandro and currently reside in Fremont. While my experience in the kitchen doesn't extend far beyond scrambling eggs and mashing together a sandwich, I still consider myself a food aficionado.
